About Me

I'm Kirk Corwin, a Computer Science student focused on data science at North Seattle College. My background in economics, art, and technical roles in bike mechanics gives me a unique, hands-on perspective on problem solving.

I build clean, reliable tools for exploring and communicating data. I work in Python, SQL, and Java, with tools like Pandas, Scikit-learn, Tableau, and Django. I have experience with machine learning workflows, data cleaning, and building user-oriented applications.

My interest is in applied AI—solving real-world problems where data and computation meet practical constraints. I thrive on cross-disciplinary projects and collaborative teams, and I’m always iterating and learning.
